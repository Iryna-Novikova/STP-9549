How to Play

  •  - The gameplay is simple and intuitive.

  • - Children draw a line between two related images—a picture and its name, two identical animals, or a shape and its outline.

  • - Correct matches are rewarded with visual confirmation and sounds, reinforcing learning.

  • - Players earn stars for completing levels and receive motivational praise after each round.

  • - The more they play, the more they unlock. The game adapts with new visuals and levels to keep children interested and challenged.
